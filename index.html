SPIDER-MAN

Spider-Man: Your Friendly Neighborhood Hero!

After an encounter with a spider, our hero, Peter Parker (aka: Spider-Man), has gained some incredible abilities. He can cling to walls, has superhuman strength, and a tingling spider-sense. He can also shoot webs from custom designed web-shooters.

Instead of abusing his powers, Spider-Man uses his powers to do good. He patrols the community he lives in and tries to help everyone that needs it. For Spider-Man, no problem is too small!

From time to time though, Spider-Man has to face off against massive villains. He has defended our neighboor several times by defeating several villains. Here is just a small list of the evil-doers Spider-Man has faced:

  • The Chameleon
  • The Vulture
  • Doctor Octopus
  • The Sandman
  • The Lizard
  • Electro
  • Mysterio
  • The Green Goblin
